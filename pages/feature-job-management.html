The Problem: Disconnected Job Tracking

Most drone operators juggle multiple tools:

  • Email threads for quotes and client communication
  • Calendar apps for scheduling
  • Spreadsheets for job status
  • Separate invoicing tools
  • Manual flight log entry after the fact

Result: Lost details, duplicated work, missed opportunities, and frustrated clients waiting for updates.

1. Capture Every Lead

Create jobs from contact form submissions, emails, or manual entry. All job details live in one place from day one.

  • Client contact information
  • Service type and scope
  • Location and site details
  • Initial quote and pricing notes

Every flight log ties back to its job. No manual reconciliation needed.
