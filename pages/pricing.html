Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What counts as a "pilot seat"?

A pilot seat is a user who can log flights, update jobs, or manage equipment. Admin/office users don't count against your seat limit.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Pay annually and save 20%. Contact us for annual pricing.

Is there a free trial?

We're currently in early access. Request access to join our pilot program with no commitment.

How is this different from spreadsheets or other tools?

ColonyCore connects every part of your operation—jobs, scheduling, flight logs, equipment, invoicing—in one system. No more switching between apps or manual data entry. Built specifically for drone service businesses.

Can I migrate my existing data?

Yes. We provide data import tools and migration support for Pro and Enterprise plans.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The mobile app works offline in the field. Data syncs automatically when you're back online.
