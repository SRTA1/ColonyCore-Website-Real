The Situation

SkyLine started with 2 pilots and grew to 6 in 18 months. But their processes didn't scale. Sarah Martinez, Operations Manager, spent 15+ hours per week juggling jobs across multiple spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, and a separate invoicing tool. Pilots weren't sure what jobs were scheduled. Invoices went out 5-7 days after completion. Cash flow was tight despite growing revenue.

The Results

  • 40% faster job completion: No more waiting for job details or missing deadlines. Pilots have clarity. Clients get updates automatically.
  • Payment time cut from 45 days to 15: Invoices sent same-day + online payment option = faster cash. 3x improvement in days-sales-outstanding.
  • Scaled to 8 drones without new admin hire: Would have needed a full-time scheduler at 8 pilots. ColonyCore handled it.
  • Better pilot satisfaction: "Our team actually knows what's happening" — less chaos, fewer surprises.

The Situation

Jessica Park's Horizon did 50+ real estate shoots per month. Details got lost: forgotten camera angles, missed address variations, unclear shot lists. Deliverables took 5-7 days. Clients asked for reshoot because something was missed. Team morale was low because of constant rework. Margins were being eaten by inefficiency.

The Solution

Implemented ColonyCore's job details and flight checklist features. Every job has a detailed shot list. Pilots confirm what they captured during the flight. No more guessing what was shot.

The Results

  • Deliverables in 2-3 days instead of 5-7: No more back-and-forth on missing shots. Real estate agents are happier. Turnaround is fast enough for same-week listing launch.
  • 50% less rework: Clear shot lists + flight confirmation = 95% first-time accuracy. Reshoot requests dropped dramatically.
  • Better client relationships: Faster delivery + fewer mistakes = happy clients. Word-of-mouth referrals improved 40%.
  • Team satisfaction improved: Pilots aren't blamed for missing shots they didn't know they needed. Clear expectations = less stress.
  • Margin improvement: Less rework overhead. Can handle 60+ shoots/month with same team size.

"ColonyCore turned our chaotic operation into a predictable machine. Our clients get their photos in 48 hours, first time, every time."
